Payout Terms

Last updated: 2026-02-25

Eligibility

Payouts are available only to verified partners who meet all platform requirements.

Payout Schedule

Payouts are processed on a monthly basis unless otherwise stated in writing.

Minimum Threshold

A minimum earnings balance may be required before a payout can be issued.

Taxes

Users are responsible for reporting and paying any applicable taxes on earnings.

Disputes

Any payout-related disputes must be submitted in writing within 30 days of the payout date.
