Feedback

Last updated: 2026-02-25

Purpose

This page allows users to share suggestions, report issues, and provide general feedback to help improve the application.

How to Submit Feedback

Use the in-app feedback submission method to send comments or suggestions. Please include as much detail as possible.

Types of Feedback

  • Feature requests
  • Bug reports
  • Usability improvements
  • General comments

Response Expectations

Feedback is reviewed periodically. While individual responses may not be provided, all submissions are considered during future updates.

Improvement Process

Feedback helps guide enhancements, prioritize fixes, and shape the long-term direction of the application.
