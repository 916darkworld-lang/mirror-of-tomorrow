Security Information

Last updated: 2026-02-25

Data Protection

The application uses standard security practices to protect user information and maintain system integrity.

Encryption

Where applicable, data transmitted between the application and backend services may be encrypted to prevent unauthorized access.

User Responsibility

Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their devices and ensuring that unauthorized individuals do not gain access to their accounts.

Account Safety

For best results, users should avoid sharing login credentials and should report any suspicious activity through the support channel.

Updates

Security practices may be updated periodically to reflect new standards, technologies, or legal requirements.
