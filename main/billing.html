Billing & Subscription Terms

Last updated: 2026-02-25

Payment Processor

All payments are processed securely through Stripe or another approved provider.

Subscription Renewals

Subscriptions renew automatically at the end of each billing cycle unless canceled by the user.

Cancellation Policy

Users may cancel their subscription at any time. Access continues until the end of the current billing period.

Refund Policy

Refunds are handled in accordance with applicable California and U.S. consumer protection laws.

Chargebacks

Unauthorized chargebacks may result in account suspension or termination.

Taxes

Applicable taxes are calculated based on the user's location and added to the subscription price when required.
