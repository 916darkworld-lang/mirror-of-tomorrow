Frequently Asked Questions

Last updated: 2026-02-25

How do I access my account?

You can access your account directly through the main application interface. If you experience issues, refer to the Support page.

How do subscriptions work?

Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled. Details are available on the Billing page.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You may cancel at any time through the in-app cancellation method. Access continues until the end of the billing cycle.

Where can I find legal information?

All legal documents are located in the main directory and linked from the footer.

What if I encounter a bug?

Please report technical issues through the Support page. Include device details and steps to reproduce the issue.
